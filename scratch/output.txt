The yfinance fallback in nse_fetcher.py handles this automatically. You will see 'yfinance Fallback' in the source field — that is not a bug. HuggingFace free inference cold-starts after inactivity. The first FinBERT call may take 20-30 seconds. All subsequent calls are fast. This is normal. GDELT occasionally returns empty responses. The cached() decorator means you serve the last good response rather than an error. Do not remove the cache. Render free tier spins down after 15 minutes of inactivity. First request after sleep takes ~30 seconds. This is a Render limitation, not a bug. What NOT To Do Do not add Bloomberg, Quandl, Alpha Vantage, Polygon.io, or any paid API. You have no budget. The free stack is sufficient for this project. Do not use localStorage or sessionStorage in React artifacts — not supported. Do not call the GDELT API more than once every 15 minutes. It will rate limit you. Do not put secrets in render.yaml. Use Render dashboard environment variables. Do not try to build the ML classification model before the data pipeline works.
